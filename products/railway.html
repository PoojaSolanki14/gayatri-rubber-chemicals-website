Indian Railways Approved Supplier

Gayatri Rubbers is a Class 1 supplier to Indian Railways, providing 65 out of 75 critical rubber components used in railway coaches. We supply to all 19 railway zones across India including ICF, MCF, and RCF. Our products meet stringent RDSO specifications and are certified for fire retardancy under EN 45545-2 HL3 standards.

Decoupling Rubber (EPDM Floor Packing)

Also known as Absorption Strip, this critical component is used for floor packing in railway coaches. Made from high-quality EPDM material, it provides excellent vibration absorption and durability.

Technical Specifications:
  • Material: EPDM Rubber
  • Annual Production Capacity: 3-3.5 Lakh Pieces
  • Compliance: RDSO Approved
  • Application: Floor packing in railway coaches

UIC Vestibule (Intercargangway Mountings)

Specialized rubber mountings for intercargangway connections between railway coaches. Approved vendor for UIC vestibule bellows, ensuring safe and comfortable passenger movement between coaches.

Technical Specifications:
  • Product: UIC Vestibule Bellows Mountings
  • Annual Production Capacity: 15,000 Sets
  • Certification: Approved Vendor Directory Listed
  • Application: Inter-coach connections
