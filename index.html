About the Company

Gayatri Rubbers & Chemicals Ltd is a Faridabad-based manufacturer and trader established in 2022, specializing in high-quality rubber profiles, compounds, and sponge rubber components. We are a strategic supplier to Indian Railways and infrastructure projects across India, providing critical components to the Automobile (EVs, commercial vehicles), Architectural (aluminum profiles for Nalco, Jindal), and Industrial sectors (sealing solutions).
