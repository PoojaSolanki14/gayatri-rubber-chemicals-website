Our Story

Gayatri Rubbers & Chemicals Ltd is a Faridabad-based manufacturer and trader established in 2022, specializing in high-quality rubber profiles, compounds, and sponge rubber components. We are a strategic supplier to Indian Railways and infrastructure projects across India, providing critical components to the Automobile (EVs, commercial vehicles), Architectural (aluminum profiles for Nalco, Jindal), and Industrial sectors (sealing solutions). As a direct supplier to Indian Railways and metro projects, we provide specialized rubber pads, gaskets, and fire-retardant neoprene products, delivering precision and quality in every solution.
